Vrai: Lab-grown diamond rings made to your size from US 3

Updated April 16, 2026

When you buy a ring off the shelf, you are choosing from what exists. Someone decided which sizes to stock, and your size may not have made the cut. Vrai works the other way around. You choose the design. You enter your size. They make it. There is no shelf. There is no leftover inventory. Your ring starts as your ring.

What This Brand Offers

Vrai is a USA brand specializing in lab-grown diamond jewelry. Rings are made to order starting at US size 3. Materials include 14K gold, 18K gold, and platinum. Prices range from $200 to $3,000. The diamonds are grown in a zero-emission foundry.

Why Made-to-Order Matters at This Size

If you wear a US 3, you have probably browsed a ring you loved and then discovered the smallest available size was 5. Made-to-order removes that dead end. Your size is entered at the beginning of the process, not patched on at the end. The ring is built around your measurement, which means the proportions — the band width, the setting height, the way the stone sits — are correct for a size 3 finger from the start.

What Petite Wearers Notice

Lab-grown diamonds are optically identical to mined ones. The difference is price. A lab-grown stone of the same carat, clarity, and cut costs less, which means you can get a larger or higher-quality stone within the same budget. For petite fingers, a slightly smaller stone often looks more balanced, so the savings can go toward a better setting or metal instead.

Practical Fit Notes

US 3 is the smallest ring size offered. No bracelets in petite sizing. The made-to-order process means delivery takes longer than a ready-to-ship ring. If you are not sure about your size, measure carefully — returns on custom pieces may have different terms.

Who This Is For

If you want a diamond ring that starts at your size rather than being resized to it, and you are comfortable with lab-grown stones, Vrai removes the usual barriers. The made-to-order model means you are not settling for what is in stock.

Bottom Line

Your ring, your size, from the first step. Lab-grown diamonds, made to order, starting at US 3. No compromises on the stone. No compromises on the fit.
