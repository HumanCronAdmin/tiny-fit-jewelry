Astrid & Miyu: stacking rings from US 3.5 in gold vermeil

Updated April 13, 2026

US 3 rings are almost mythical. Stores stock US 5-7 as the smallest, or worse -- adjustable sizing that swallows a petite finger whole. Astrid & Miyu’s smallest ring size is US 3.5. That’s not a typo. For someone hunting size 2 rings in every other store, that number arrives like a whisper: we see you.

They’re a UK brand. Materials are gold vermeil, silver, and gold plated. Prices range from £30 to £200.

That US 3.5 ring size matters because it lands in a gap most brands ignore. Smaller sizes exist, but they’re either vintage, handmade, or priced like heirlooms. Astrid & Miyu’s modern, stackable designs let you build a delicate set without hunting estate sales. You can layer two or three thin gold vermeil bands on the same finger and it looks intentional, not cluttered. On a small hand, that proportion is everything.

This is the brand for petite wearers who want modern, stackable ring designs without resorting to custom or vintage. If you’re US 3.5 or above, their rings will fit. The price range sits in the accessible luxury zone -- gold vermeil and silver from £30 to £200. Not disposable, not precious. Just right for everyday stacking.
