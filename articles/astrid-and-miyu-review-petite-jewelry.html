Astrid & Miyu: stacking rings from US 3.5 in gold vermeil

Updated April 16, 2026

If you wear a US size 3.5 ring, most stores have already told you no — not explicitly, but by starting their sizing at US 5 or US 4. Astrid & Miyu starts at US 3.5. That number matters when your fingers have been falling through the gap between "standard" and "available."

Astrid & Miyu is a UK brand built around the art of stacking. They use gold vermeil, silver, and gold plated materials, with prices generally between $30 and $200. It is not impulse jewelry, but it is not an heirloom either. It sits in the range where building a collection feels realistic.

More Than Just a Size: The Joy of Options

A US 3.5 ring size is a starting point. It opens up stacking in a way that most petite wearers have never had access to. For years, jewelry choices were limited to whatever rare piece stayed on the finger. There was no "collection," no intentional layering. Just one ring, if any.

With a range of US 3.5 options, that changes. You can layer two or three delicate gold vermeil bands on a single finger, and they look exactly as they should — intentional and harmonious, not bulky or ill-fitting. On a small hand, proportions are everything. It is about seeing the jewelry, not just the gap where it should be.

The Stacking Approach

Stacking is about having choices. Some days one ring says everything it needs to. Other days the appeal is in the combination — a gold vermeil band next to a silver one, a plain ring alongside one with a small detail. Astrid & Miyu’s catalog is wide enough at US 3.5 to build that kind of variety without repeating the same piece twice.

The designs are built to be worn together. Matching textures, complementary widths, pieces conceived as a set rather than as isolated items. At US 3.5, you have enough in their range to stack without running out of options.

Practical Fit Notes

The materials — gold vermeil, silver, and gold plated — sit at an accessible price point for the quality. The price range, from $30 to $200, makes building a collection piece by piece realistic rather than a single large commitment.

Bracelets from Astrid & Miyu start at 15cm. Fit below 14cm has not been confirmed by the brand. If wrist sizing is a priority, check individual product pages carefully before purchasing.

The brand’s strength for petite wearers is in rings, specifically the stacking range starting at US 3.5. That is where the consistent small-size availability sits.
