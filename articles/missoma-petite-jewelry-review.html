Missoma: Layering jewelry that covers your whole frame

Updated April 16, 2026

Sizing a ring is one measurement. Sizing a bracelet is another. But what about a necklace that hits your sternum instead of your collarbone? Or a choker that gapes at the back of your neck? When you are petite, the sizing problem is not just your fingers — it is your whole frame. Missoma is one of the few brands that addresses all three.

What This Brand Offers

Missoma is a London-based brand focused on layering. Rings start at US 4. Bracelets start at 15cm and are not confirmed to fit wrists under 14cm. Necklaces come with extender chains and multiple clasp points. Materials are 18K gold vermeil and silver. Prices range from $50 to $400.

Why Full-Frame Coverage Matters

Most petite jewelry brands solve one problem: the ring or the bracelet. Missoma addresses the necklace too. If you are under 155cm, a standard 45cm necklace does not sit where it was designed to sit. Missoma's chain necklaces with multiple clasp points let you shorten the drop. You are not cutting chain or adding a makeshift knot — the adjustment is built in.

What Petite Wearers Notice

The layering approach works well for small frames because thin chains and slim bands do not overwhelm your proportions. Stacking three thin pieces looks intentional rather than heavy. The gold vermeil has a warmth that reads as fine jewelry from a distance, at a price point that makes layering multiple pieces realistic.

Practical Fit Notes

Rings at US 4 are not the smallest available — this is not the brand for very small ring fingers. Bracelets start at 15cm and are not confirmed to reach below 14cm. The necklace adjustability is where Missoma earns its petite relevance for smaller frames. Stick to the chain necklaces with multiple clasp points.

Who This Is For

If you want to layer rings, bracelets, and necklaces from one brand, and you want all three to fit your petite frame without modification, Missoma is one of the only options. This is a brand for the whole silhouette, not just one hand.

Bottom Line

Rings, bracelets, necklaces — all adjustable, all from one place, all designed to layer. If your sizing problem extends past your fingers to your whole frame, Missoma takes that seriously.
