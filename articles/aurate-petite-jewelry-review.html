AUrate: Modern fine jewelry rings from US size 3

Updated April 16, 2026

Most fine jewelry brands start their ring sizing at US 5. A few reach US 4. AUrate goes to US 3. That number matters when your finger has been falling through the gap between "standard" and "available." AUrate is a NYC-based brand where modern, everyday fine jewelry comes in a size that fits you.

What This Brand Offers

AUrate is a NYC-based fine jewelry brand. Rings start at US size 3. Materials are 14K gold, 18K gold, and some diamond pieces. Prices range from $50 to $600. The aesthetic is minimalist and modern — pieces that work on a Tuesday morning and a Friday evening.

Why US 3 Matters in Fine Jewelry

Fine jewelry at US 3 is hard to find without a custom order. AUrate stocks it as a standard option. You pick the ring, pick your size, and check out. No emails to customer service. No "we'll see if we can do that." The rings are designed with proportions that suit a smaller hand — thin bands, low-profile settings, and stacking designs that look airy rather than heavy when you layer three of them on one finger.

What Petite Wearers Notice

The minimalist designs look proportionate on small hands without appearing oversized. At $50 to $600, the range is wide enough that both an everyday stackable and a special-occasion piece are realistic purchases. The $50 entry point means you can start building a collection without treating every ring as a major decision.

Practical Fit Notes

Rings at US 3 are available across most styles, but check individual product pages for confirmation. Not every design reaches size 3. Bracelets start at 15cm and are not suitable for wrists under 14cm.

Who This Is For

If you want modern fine jewelry at US 3 without the custom-order process, and you like clean, minimalist designs that stack well on small hands, AUrate fills that spot. The price range makes it realistic for everyday wear, not just special occasions.

Bottom Line

Rings from US 3. Modern fine jewelry from $50. Designs that look right on a petite hand without needing to be scaled down. AUrate treats your size as standard, not special.
