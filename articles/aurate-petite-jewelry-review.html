What you get with AUrate

AUrate is a USA-based brand. They focus on modern fine, everyday jewelry. You'll find pieces made with 14K gold, 18K gold, and genuine diamonds. Their price range is $50 to $600. That's broad enough to find something for a first purchase or a more significant addition to your collection.

Right now, we're only talking about their rings. Their other categories, like bracelets, typically don't cater to smaller wrist sizes. AUrate's bracelets start at 15cm, which means they won't fit wrists smaller than that. So, for petite hands, we're focusing on what works: their rings.

The style is clean and understated. Think thin bands, low-profile settings, and designs that stack without looking bulky. It’s jewelry that blends into your life, rather than demanding attention. You can wear AUrate rings everywhere—to work, running errands, out to dinner. They don't snag on clothes or feel heavy, which really matters when you have smaller hands and anything oversized feels like too much.
