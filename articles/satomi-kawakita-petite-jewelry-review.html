Satomi Kawakita: Handmade 18K rings from US size 2

Updated April 16, 2026

There is a difference between a brand that adds small sizes to its catalog and a jeweler who understands small hands because she has lived among them her whole life. Satomi Kawakita is a Japanese jeweler based in New York City. Her rings start at US size 2 through custom orders, and each one is made by hand in 18K gold.

What This Brand Offers

Handcrafted rings and fine jewelry in 18K gold. Custom sizing down to US 2. Prices range from $200 to $2,000. Based in NYC with international shipping. Each piece has an organic, slightly irregular texture that comes from being shaped by hand rather than cast from a mold.

Why This Brand Feels Different

When a Japanese designer makes jewelry in a market dominated by Western sizing, the result is different. Japanese ring sizes start much smaller than American ones. That awareness shows up in the proportions. The bands are thin enough for a size 2 finger without looking fragile. The settings sit close to the hand rather than towering above it. These are design choices that come from familiarity, not from reading a size chart.

What Petite Wearers Notice

The organic texture gives each ring a sense of being one-of-a-kind. On a small hand, this works in your favor — the slight variations in surface catch light differently depending on the angle. Petite wearers who are used to polished, machine-made rings notice the warmth of the finish right away. It feels like something a person made, because it is.

Practical Fit Notes

Custom sizing requires contacting the brand directly. This is not a click-and-ship experience. You will need your exact measurement, and there will be a wait. Bracelets do not have specific petite sizing. This brand is strongest for rings.

Who This Is For

If you want a ring that was made by someone who did not need to be told that US 2 is a real size, and you prefer organic textures over polished perfection, Satomi Kawakita is worth the wait. The price reflects handmade 18K gold, not mass production.

Bottom Line

Handmade in NYC by a Japanese jeweler. Custom sizing to US 2. 18K gold with an organic touch. This is jewelry made by hands that understand yours.
