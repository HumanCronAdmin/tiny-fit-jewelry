Agete: Delicate Gold for Petite Fingers

Updated April 09, 2026

Agete holds a particular charm for those with petite hands. Their jewelry, born in Japan, carries a delicate, vintage-inspired air. Pieces feel carefully considered, never overwhelming.

Each design seems to whisper a story, a quiet elegance that suits everyday wear. It is the kind of jewelry that becomes a part of you, rather than simply an accessory.

For genuinely small fingers, Agete is a rare find. Rings begin at a tiny US size 1.5, which is a Japanese size 1. This range means even the slenderest fingers can find a perfect fit, without needing to compromise on style or scale. The intricate details, often inspired by antique motifs, are scaled down thoughtfully. They do not look lost on a small hand. Instead, they enhance it.

Bracelets start at 14cm. For some, this might be just right. For wrists under 14cm, it could be a snug fit, or perhaps a bit loose. The designs are delicate enough not to feel bulky, even if the length is a slight consideration.

The materials reflect this attention to detail. Agete uses 10K and 18K gold, alongside silver. The price range, from around $80 to $500, places it in a mid-luxury category. It feels like an investment in something that will last, both in material and timeless design.

There is one significant consideration for those outside Japan. Agete ships only within Japan. This means direct online purchase from abroad is not possible.

Who is this brand for?

This brand is for someone who appreciates subtle beauty. A person who seeks jewelry with a story, a vintage soul, but crafted for modern wear. It is for those who have struggled to find rings small enough to fit their fingers properly. Someone who values fine materials and delicate craftsmanship. If a piece of jewelry should feel like a second skin, Agete understands this.

Sizing Details

Agete’s ring sizing is a standout feature. Their smallest ring size is JP size 1, which translates to a US size 1.5. This is genuinely petite. For bracelets, the minimum length is 14cm. While this might be suitable for some smaller wrists, it is important to measure carefully if your wrist circumference is less than 14cm. The designs are not adjustable.

How to Buy

Since Agete offers only domestic shipping within Japan, purchasing requires a bit more planning. The most direct way is to visit one of their boutiques if traveling to Japan. This allows for trying on pieces, especially their precise JP size 1 rings. For those unable to travel, a proxy shopping service is the only option. These services purchase items on your behalf in Japan and then ship them internationally. It adds a layer of complexity and cost, but it makes their unique pieces accessible.

If the delicate, vintage-inspired aesthetic of Agete appeals to you, and you are exploring other Japanese brands with similar sizing considerations, Nojess might also be worth a look.
