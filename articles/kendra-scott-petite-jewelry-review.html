What to Keep in Mind

It’s important to be clear about sizing with Kendra Scott. If your ring size is US 3, or your wrist circumference is 13cm, this brand won't be the right fit for you. Their rings start at a US 4, and bracelets at 15cm. We're always upfront about these limitations because finding jewelry that fits is the whole point. There’s no point in speculating if an adjustable piece *might* fit smaller. It’s designed for 15cm and up, or US 4 and up. Period. While Kendra Scott does offer adjustable options, it's worth noting what that means for you. Sometimes, those adjustable cords or chains can leave a long, dangling bit of material once you've tightened them to your desired fit. You know how annoying that can be, getting caught on things or just feeling awkward. So, if adjustability is a key factor for you, take a close look at the specific design. Some styles might have a more integrated adjustable mechanism, while others might result in that extra length you'd rather avoid. The best approach is to always check the product details and photos to see if it aligns with your preference for how an adjustable piece should look and feel.
