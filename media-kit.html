Our Audience

Our visitors are women actively searching for jewelry that fits their petite frame. They are not browsing — they are ready to buy, and they come to us because mainstream jewelry sites do not serve their size.

  • Primary demographic: Women ages 20-45 with ring sizes US 3 or smaller and/or wrist circumference 14cm or smaller
  • Intent: High purchase intent — visitors are actively looking for specific sizes
  • Geography: Primarily USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and Japan
  • Channels: Organic search (Google), Pinterest, Reddit, X (Twitter)
