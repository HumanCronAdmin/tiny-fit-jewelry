PRODUCT HUNT LAUNCH COPY - TinyFit Jewelry
============================================

1. TAGLINE
----------
Finally, jewelry that fits tiny hands and wrists.


2. DESCRIPTION
--------------
TinyFit Jewelry is a free, curated database of 31 verified brands that
carry ring sizes 2-4 and bracelets for wrists under 14cm. No more
guessing which brands go small enough -- every listing is size-verified
so you can shop with confidence. Includes Japanese artisan brands that
most Western shoppers have never heard of.


3. MAKER COMMENT (first comment)
---------------------------------
Hi Product Hunt!

I built TinyFit Jewelry because I got tired of the same frustrating
cycle: find a beautiful ring online, fall in love with it, check the
size chart -- smallest available is size 5. Close the tab. Repeat.

I am 153cm tall. My ring size is 3. My wrist is 13.5cm around. Standard
"small" jewelry does not fit me. It is not even close.

After years of this, I started keeping a personal list of brands that
actually carry my sizes. I discovered that Japanese jewelry makers are
far ahead on this -- many of them craft down to size 1 as standard
practice, not as a special order. But finding these brands from outside
Japan is not easy, and most English-language jewelry guides completely
ignore them.

So I turned my personal list into a proper database. Every brand has
been verified: I checked their size charts, confirmed availability, and
noted the actual size ranges they carry. No affiliate links, no
sponsored placements. Just 31 brands that genuinely serve small hands
and wrists.

If you have ever typed "tiny ring sizes" into a search bar at 2am, this
is for you.
