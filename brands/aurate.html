AUrate: Affordable Fine Jewelry from NYC by Female Founders

USA · ring & bracelet · $50–$600 · NYC, women-founded

Verified by TinyFit
14K/18K solid gold recycled gold made in NYC
Shop AUrate

AUrate is a New York fine jewelry brand built around one idea: solid gold priced to wear every day, not saved for weddings. The two women co-founders launched it on the argument that the same quality of gold shouldn't cost several times more just because of the label. Here's a look at the story, the materials, and the sizing.

What AUrate Stands For

AUrate was started in New York by two women, Sophie Kahn from the Netherlands and Bouchra Ezzahraoui from Morocco, who have been friends since graduate school. On their official site, they describe themselves as "Fine jewelry, born and raised in N.Y.C".

Materials and Local NYC Manufacturing

AUrate uses 14K and 18K solid gold, recycled gold, and conflict-free diamonds that comply with the Kimberley Process. All manufacturing happens right in NYC. Their official site states, "Our gold is sustainable, casted, polished and perfected by generational craftsmen," showing their commitment to materials. Their products also come with a lifetime warranty.

Price: $50 to $600

The price range is from $50 to $600. AUrate's motto is "luxury without the markup," and they keep prices down by manufacturing directly in NYC and cutting out the middleman.

Sizing

Rings start at US size 1. US size 2 and US size 3 are both available for petite fingers. Bracelets start at 6 inches (about 15.2 cm) at the smallest, so wrists 14 cm or smaller don't have a fit here.

Frequently Asked

Where is the jewelry made?
Everything is made in New York City. They choose materials like recycled gold and conflict-free diamonds that comply with the Kimberley Process.
Is AUrate's gold plated?
No, it's not. They use 14K or 18K solid gold, so you don't have to worry about it fading or peeling.
Can I wear it every day without taking it off?
Since it's solid gold, it's generally fine to leave on, even in the shower or during a workout. To keep it shining, we recommend occasionally wiping it with a soft cloth or gently washing it with soap and water.
Is there a warranty?
Yes, AUrate's jewelry comes with a lifetime warranty. Their official site states, "they're guaranteed for life".

Rings go down to US size 1, so petite fingers have real options here. NYC local manufacturing, recycled gold, and a lifetime warranty are part of the package. Bracelets, though, don't have sizes for wrists 14cm or smaller.
