Why we list AUrate

AUrate is a strong brand for petite women looking for modern, everyday fine jewelry that fits. Their rings are reliably available from US size 3, making them accessible for even the smallest fingers without needing special orders. While their bracelets often state a 15cm minimum, many designs incorporate adjustable features, allowing for a surprisingly good fit on wrists slightly under 14cm. They offer beautiful, minimalist pieces that look proportionate and feel comfortable on a petite frame.

Size Availability

Rings: Available from US size 3. Size 3 is available, which is smaller than most mainstream brands.

Bracelets: Available from 15cm. You may need the smallest setting or an adjustable style for wrists under 14cm.

Adjustable: Yes. Some pieces can be adjusted to fit smaller sizes.
