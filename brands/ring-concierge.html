Ring Concierge: NY-Based Fine Jewelry Designed by a Woman

USA · ring & bracelet · $200–$10,000 · NYC, bespoke bridal

Verified by TinyFit
14K/18K solid gold diamonds NYC
Shop Ring Concierge

Ring Concierge is fine jewelry you can put on your hand before buying — unusual for a brand with a serious bespoke bridal business. Four physical stores (NYC Soho, Madison Avenue, LA, Houston, Boca Raton) mean an engagement ring consultation happens face to face, and an everyday gold chain can be tried on and walked out with the same afternoon. Founded by Nicole Wegman in 2013, solo, in an industry that was almost entirely male-led. Here's a look at the story, the materials, and who the sizing works for.

What Ring Concierge Stands For

Nicole Wegman founded the brand by herself in 2013. The official website states, "In an industry that has been historically dominated by men, Nicole is disrupting with her vision." The brand describes its position as "the leading luxury jeweler committed to designing for women, by women."

Materials and Collection

The jewelry is mainly 14K and 18K solid gold. They handle both natural and lab-grown diamonds. The collection is extensive, ranging from custom bridal rings to easy-to-wear fine jewelry. They don't disclose their material sourcing.

Physical Stores and Buying Experience

They have physical stores in New York's SoHo and Madison Avenue, Los Angeles, Houston, and Boca Raton. You can get a consultation for a custom engagement ring or try on pieces from the collection in person.

Price: $200 to $10,000

The price range is wide, from around $200 to over $10,000. It covers everything from everyday items to custom engagement rings, so you can choose based on your budget.

Sizing

Rings start at US size 2. US size 2 and US size 3 are both available for petite fingers. The Mini Diamond Tennis Bracelet adjusts from 5.5 inches (~14cm), making it one of the few options that fits a 14cm wrist. Most other bracelets start at 6 inches (~15cm), which is too long for wrists 14cm or smaller.

♡ What makes Ring Concierge distinctive

Founded by a woman in a male-dominated industry, Ring Concierge designs fine jewelry for women. They offer a wide range from everyday solid gold pieces to custom engagement rings.

⚠ Good to know before you buy

You get solid 14K/18K gold, not plated. Rings start at US size 2, but check bracelet sizing carefully—most start at 6 inches, which is too large for 14cm wrists.

Frequently Asked

What are the materials? Is it plated?
They use 14K or 18K solid gold. It's not plated or vermeil, so you don't have to worry about the color wearing off.
Are the diamonds natural?
They handle both natural and lab-grown diamonds. Which one you get depends on the product or your custom consultation.
Where is it made?
The official website doesn't specifically disclose manufacturing locations or material sources.
Can I only buy in-store?
The online store ships worldwide. Of course, you can also see the pieces in person at their physical stores in New York, Los Angeles, Houston, and Boca Raton.
