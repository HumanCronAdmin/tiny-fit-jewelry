Rings from US Size 2

Their rings start at US size 2. So even if you have slender fingers, you can find the perfect size. The designs are very minimal, so they blend naturally with your everyday style. They're great for stacking or wearing alone for a simple look.

What Automic Gold Stands For

On their official site, Automic Gold defines themselves like this: "Automic Gold is a queer, trans-owned fine jewelry company that creates timeless, genderless pieces." This means the brand is queer and trans-owned, creating fine jewelry that isn't bound by time or gender.

Their size range is also a key feature. The official site states, "we are still the only fine jewelry brand to carry inclusive sizes from 2-16". While it's hard to verify if they are truly the only one, it's certainly rare to find a brand that offers such a wide range from US size 2 to 16. The designs are thoroughly minimal, with no extra decoration.

Materials and Craftsmanship

All their pieces are made from 14K solid gold. Since it's gold all the way through, you don't have to worry about the surface peeling off like with plated jewelry. Their official care guide even says, "These metals aren't afraid of water or chemicals, so you can wear them every day!". Everything is handmade in New York using recycled gold and reclaimed or ethically sourced stones.
