Why we list Bloom

Bloom offers an excellent entry point into petite jewelry, especially for those on a budget. As an affordable Japanese brand, they provide rings from US size 2 and bracelets from 14cm, making stylish everyday pieces accessible for smaller dimensions. Their casual, everyday style is perfect for those seeking versatile jewelry that won't break the bank. While their shipping is limited to Japan, their price point makes them a great option for exploring petite sizing without a investment.

Size Availability

Rings: Available from US size 2. This is one of the few brands that carries sizes this small for adult women.

Bracelets: Available from 14cm. Fits a 14cm wrist snugly but will be loose on wrists smaller than 14cm.

Best for Petite Women

Everyday Stacking Rings

Their affordable rings, available from US size 2, are perfect for building a delicate, everyday stack on petite fingers.

Simple Chain Bracelets

Starting at 14cm, these basic and versatile bracelets are a good option for a casual fit on smaller wrists.

💡 Petite Tip

Given their affordable price point, Bloom is ideal for experimenting with different petite styles. Consider trying a ring in US size 2 to confirm your fit before investing in more expensive pieces from other brands.
