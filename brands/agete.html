Why we list Agete

Agete is a dream for petite fingers, offering rings from a tiny US size 1.5 (JP size 1). Their signature delicate, vintage-inspired designs are perfectly scaled, ensuring they don't overwhelm smaller hands. While their bracelets start at 14cm, which might be a snug fit for some under 14cm wrists, their ring selection is exceptional. The main limitation is their Japan-only domestic shipping, meaning a trip to Japan or a proxy service is required for purchase.

♡ What's great

For someone who has consistently been overlooked by mainstream jewelry brands, finding Agete offers a profound sense of validation and belonging. It's the joy of finally wearing rings that fit perfectly without spinning or falling off, allowing for real self-expression. This brand provides the rare and freeing experience of feeling seen and catered to in the world of fine jewelry.

⚠ What to watch for

While Agete's ring sizes are exceptional for petite fingers, shoppers with wrists under 14cm should be aware that their bracelets typically start at this length. This means bracelets might still be too loose for some very small wrists, potentially sliding off or requiring professional adjustment. It's to measure wrist circumference precisely, as the delicate chains may not be easily shortened without affecting the design or integrity.

Size Availability

Rings: Available from US size 1.5. This is one of the few brands that carries sizes this small for adult women.

Bracelets: Available from 14cm. This works for wrists under 14cm without modification.
