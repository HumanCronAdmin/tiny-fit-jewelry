enewton is a family-run bracelet brand out of Atlanta. Gold-filled construction means the pieces are durable enough to wear constantly — shower, workout, sleep, all fine. Elizabeth Newton started the brand in 2011 around the idea of meaningful gifts you can give at reachable prices. Here's a look at the sizes, the materials, and who the fit works for.

What enewton Stands For

Founded in 2011 in Atlanta, Georgia, by Elizabeth Newton and her husband Ben, enewton is a family-owned brand. Their official website states, "From the beginning, friends and family were the focus of the brand." The brand's very first piece was a turquoise cross necklace.

Materials: Gold-Filled Beads

The main materials are gold-filled beads, natural stone beads, and hematine. While not solid gold, they're durable enough for everyday wear. The official care guide says, "you can shower, sweat, swim and sleep in your enewton!"

Price: $30 to $150

With a focus on bracelets, the price range is from $30 to $150. This makes their pieces quite accessible.

Sizing

enewton specializes in bracelets and doesn't carry rings. Bracelets come in three size lines:
- EGirl: 5.5 inches (~14 cm). Designed for smaller wrists and teens. This fits 14cm wrists.
- Standard: 6.25 inches (~16 cm)
- Extends: 7.25 inches (~18.5 cm)

♡ What makes enewton distinctive

enewton specializes in durable, gold-filled bracelets you can wear 24/7. They offer a dedicated "EGirl" petite line at 5.5 inches (~14 cm), which is rare for fashion jewelry brands.

⚠ Good to know before you buy

The jewelry is gold-filled, not solid gold, so avoid chlorine and chemicals. Their stretch bracelets are not adjustable, so be sure to choose the right size before you buy.

Frequently Asked

Where is enewton jewelry made?
It's designed and assembled in Atlanta, Georgia.
What is gold-filled material?
It's a material made by heat- and pressure-bonding a layer of gold to a base metal like brass. The gold layer is thicker than gold plating, which makes it more durable.
Is it really okay to wear it all the time?
The official website says you can wear it in the shower, while sweating, swimming, and sleeping. However, it's best to avoid chemicals and chlorine, as they can cause discoloration.
Can the bracelet size be adjusted?
Most of their pieces are stretch bracelets made with beads, so they can't be individually adjusted. Be sure to choose the right size for you from the three lines (EGirl, Standard, Extends) before you buy.
