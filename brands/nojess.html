Why We Recommend Nojess

Nojess, a sister brand to Agete, is another excellent Japanese option for petite jewelry lovers, particularly for rings. They consistently offer rings from US size 1.5 (JP size 1), ideal for the smallest fingers, and their antique, romantic style lends itself well to delicate scaling. While their bracelets start at 14cm, which might be slightly loose for the very smallest wrists, their ring selection is a definite highlight. Remember, like many Japanese brands, shipping is domestic-only.
