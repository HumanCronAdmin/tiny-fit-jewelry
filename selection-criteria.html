How we choose what goes on this site

We built TinyFit for people with small hands. So the list here is picky on purpose: we only include brands and shops that treat small sizes as a real thing, not an afterthought.

What goes in the Main Database

A brand belongs in the Main Database when three things are true.

1
It's an independent brand.

One company designs, makes, and sells the jewelry. Marketplaces, curators, and individual sellers go in a different section below.

2
It carries small sizes.

Rings from US 4 or smaller. Bracelets from 14cm or smaller. We only list bracelet sizing that has been confirmed to fit petite wrists — 15cm bracelets labeled "adjustable" without proof don't qualify.

3
You can buy from it.

We lean toward brands that ship internationally so people anywhere can shop. When a brand only ships to one region and is the only place readers there can find small sizes, it still goes in — that's why Japan-only Japanese brands are here for friends living in Japan.

♡ Community Recommendations

This is where TinyFit friends bring back what they've tried themselves.

What's here isn't an independent brand — it's a curated marketplace where you can search across many indie designers, an Etsy artisan making one-of-a-kind pieces, an affordable shop that ships almost anywhere. Different shape, same mission.

People in our community tried these with their own hands and came back saying "this one worked for me." Those voices are what this section is made of.

When we pass on something

Sometimes someone recommends a shop and we look into it and decide it isn't right for the list. Usually because:

When you look closer, the small sizes aren't there, and the only answer is "we'll resize it after purchase"
It's dropshipping or reselling, not original design
The quality or ethics are something we don't want to pass on to friends

This list moves

New brands get added when we find them. Brands move into Community Recommendations when they no longer fit the main criteria. What we care about is whether this is useful to the people who come here looking for something that fits.
